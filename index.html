10-Pack Yonkey Monkey Skein Tencel Yarn - 70% Bamboo, 30% Cotton (Pink 9025)

Introducing Yonkey Monkey Bamboo Cotton Yarn, your perfect choice for crafting garments and accessories for children. This exquisite yarn combines the luxurious qualities of bamboo and cotton, offering a soft, cuddly texture that turns every knitting project into a treasured keepsake. Whether you're creating cozy clothing or charming accessories, this yarn is your ideal companion.


Key Features:

  • Premium Blend: Crafted from a superior blend of 70% bamboo and 30% cotton, this yarn boasts natural fibers of exceptional quality, ensuring a delightful crafting experience. Plus, it's budget-friendly!
  • Naturally Soft: Each skein weighs a mere 50g, making it lightweight and delightfully soft to the touch. Our cotton fiber undergoes a special treatment, resulting in a wide array of beautiful colors to choose from.
  • Baby-Friendly: Rest assured that this yarn is hypoallergenic and safe for even newborns. The bamboo fibers offer added UV protection, making it ideal for baby clothes.
  • Breathable Material: With its excellent breathability and moisture-absorbing properties, this yarn is perfect for crafting summer wear, socks, and more. It's an excellent alternative for those with wool allergies.
  • Ease of Use: Enjoy a frustration-free knitting experience as this yarn doesn't snag, fray, or split easily. It works best with 3-4 US size needles and boasts a generous length of up to 210 meters per 6-ply skein, with a diameter of 0.047 inches.
  • Versatile Crafting: Perfect for a wide range of projects, from baby garments, clothing, and accessories to socks, gloves, headdresses, toys, jewelry, and beyond. It excels in both knitting and crocheting and is suitable for hand sewing.
  • Low Maintenance: This yarn is durable, easy-care, and versatile. It can be conveniently machine washed and dried, ensuring hassle-free upkeep for your projects.
  • Eco-Friendly: Free from harmful chemicals and crafted with nature in mind, it's a sustainable choice for eco-conscious crafters.
